About

One Free DJ is a freestyle DJ who's passion for diverse music genres led him to experiment with different styles, from drum'n'bass and breakbeat to jazz-hop, reggae and house, and everything in between. He honed his skills as a DJ by playing at local venues and parties, perfecting his craft with each performance.

Over time, One Free DJ developed a unique sound that blends different genres seamlessly, creating a distinct musical experience for his audience, making him a sought-after performer for clubs and festivals alike.

One Free DJ's style is characterized by a deep understanding of the music he plays, combined with an instinctive feel for the rhythm and flow of the crowd. His passion for music and his dedication to his craft continue to drive his success as a freestyle DJ.
